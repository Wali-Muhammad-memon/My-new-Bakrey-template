About Us

Established in 1998, Hobnob revolutionized the concept of the bakery chain in Karachi by introducing a culture of innovation in product development and providing easy access with branches in every neighborhood across the city. Over the last 20 years we’ve expanded to become the largest bakery retail network in the city and we’re not done yet – in fact, we’re growing every day! Our mission is to give our customers the most authentic and artisanal bakery experience at the best price. All our products – whether it’s bread, biscuits, sandwiches, cakes or brownies – are freshly baked every day using the best natural ingredients and time-honored processes. We know our customers are deeply concerned about what goes into their food so we work hard to ensure that every batch is baked from scratch and is preservative- and additive-free as far as possible. Come on over and experience how we’re baking it better.

NEW CHILLS FOR SUMMER

By Admin on November 28, 2023

Sweetness has had a role in human nutrition, helping to orient feeding behavior toward foods providing both energy and essential nutrients. Infants and young children in particular base many of their food choices on familiarity and sweet taste.

The enjoyment you feel from eating something sweet is facilitated by the same morphine-like biochemical systems in the brain that are thought to be the basis for all highly-rewarding activities. From an evolutionary standpoint, our survival depends on our ability to take in energy from our diet.
